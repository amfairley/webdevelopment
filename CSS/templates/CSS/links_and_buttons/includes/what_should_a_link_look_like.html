In order to provide a good user experience, links should follow clear and consistent conventions across the website. So however you decide to style your links, keep the same type of links looking the same.

They should be easily distinguishable from regular text, often through colour, underlining, or other visual cues such as the cursor becoming a pointer when hovered over.
